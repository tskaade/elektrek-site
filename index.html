we @ elektrek built our machines based on the premise that we needed something more hardcore than what is available on the market — being mature tech / enduro / motocross / two-wheeled enthusiasts — & get us back into the single track with our families & colleagues. Knowing we will hit rough terrain on work sites, mixed shield and deep woods conditions, but also allow us to commute (rural to urban) at the same time.

  • Stealth and performance
  • Supports industrial / commercial / recreational applications
  • Long lasting — components and structure
  • Suitable for all conditions
  • Has a useable crank and pedals
  • Stackable proven technology — a platform we build off of and upgrade as newer tech arrives
  • Repairable by a user in the field
  • Reduced complexity while maintaining quality & reliability
  • Supports in-city-limit ministry guidelines
  • Unlockable for private or rural applications
