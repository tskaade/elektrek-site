How the verdict works
Too light· under 12 kt — foil or beer o'clock
Rideable· 12–16 kt — big kite, cruisy

SEND IT· 22–30 kt — booties, boost, grin

⚠ Disclaimer — read this

This is a hobby app, not a certified safety or weather service. Wind models and a single buoy can be wrong, offline, or hours stale. The verdicts here are guidance for fun, not a green light to launch.

Kiteboarding is a high-risk sport. Always eyeball the actual water before rigging, know your own gear's wind range and your own skill limits, wear a helmet and impact vest, never kite alone, and have a self-rescue plan. Offshore (west) wind can blow you to the far shore — treat it as a hard no without boat support.

You alone are responsible for your go/no-go call. The maker of this app accepts no liability for any decision made using it. When in doubt, don't go out — ask a certified instructor at ukiteboard.com.
